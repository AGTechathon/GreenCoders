Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users:

Information you provide:

  • Account information (name, email, phone)
  • Food donation details (type, quantity, location)
  • Communication with other users

Information we collect automatically:

  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • Device information (browser type, IP address)
  • Location data (if enabled)

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Connect food donors with recipients
  • Ensure food safety and compliance
  • Communicate with users about their posts
  • Analyze usage to improve our platform
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Information Sharing

We may share information:

  • Between users: To facilitate food donations (contact info, location)
  • With service providers: Who assist in operating our platform
  • For legal reasons: When required by law or to protect rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Access controls

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Update or correct your account information
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Disable location services
  • Delete your account (contact us)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Remember preferences
  • Analyze site traffic

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify users of significant changes.
